What is MOTIV?

The MOTIV™ Development Platform for Android is designed to simplify haptic integration for both OEMs and application developers. The first development platform of its kind—MOTIV ushers in a new era of customizable touch effects to drive next-gen UIs.

MOTIV consists of two core elements: 1) The MOTIV Integrator is a tool for OEMs that quickly injects tactile feedback into the OEM build, and 2) The MOTIV SDK helps application developers to easily design their apps with haptics. Learn More ►

What is Haptics?

Haptics (touch feedback) is the future of the user experience in digital devices.

Why? Touch is at the core of personal experience and the only sense capable of simultaneous input and output.

Touch feedback improves task performance, increases user satisfaction, and supplies a greater sense of realism and enjoyment. ►
